Shopping Baltimore

My final project is an educational game and data visualization of shopping at Baltimore farmer's markets on snap benefits. The point of the game is to demonstrate why it is difficult for people on SNAP benefits to buy local, organic food. I focused on Baltimore because I am from Maryland and it is close to my heart.

Sketches

Players navigate through the game using the keyboard to switch between "modes." Points are recieved by buying food on the grocery list with SNAP money -- this is done by clicking on the food sprites.
